About
"It's a lot of fun to come up with my own creations of food, and friends and family always appreciate homecooked meals" -Sabrina Chen, John Hopkins University, '19
Aiming to connect college students around the nation through a shared love of food, FoodHaCs is created by students for students. In our busy college lives, we don't have much time to prepare meals for ourselves.To help out, we've put together a collection of simple but tasty recipes so you can treat your body while satisfying your tastebuds.
